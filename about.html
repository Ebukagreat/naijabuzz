About NaijaBuzz

NaijaBuzz is Nigeria's fast-growing digital media platform, dedicated to delivering timely, accurate, and engaging content across the topics that matter most to Nigerians and Africans worldwide.

We cover Tech & AI, Finance, Health & Lifestyle, Entertainment, and Nigerian/African News — giving our readers a one-stop destination for everything they need to stay informed, inspired, and ahead.

Founded in Lagos, Nigeria, NaijaBuzz is built by Nigerians, for Nigerians — and for the global African community that wants real stories told with authenticity and pride.

"To inform, educate, and entertain Africans everywhere — one story at a time."

What We Cover

Tech & AI — The latest in technology, artificial intelligence, gadgets, and digital tools relevant to Nigerian and African audiences.

Finance — Personal finance, investing, the Nigerian economy, forex, and practical money advice for everyday Nigerians.

Health & Lifestyle — Evidence-based health information, wellness tips, and lifestyle content tailored to Nigerian realities.

Entertainment — Nollywood, Afrobeats, celebrity news, and the booming Nigerian creative economy.

Naija / Africa — News, analysis, and stories about Nigeria and the broader African continent.
