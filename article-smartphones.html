Best Budget Smartphones in Nigeria Under ₦100,000 in 2026

You do not need to spend ₦500,000 to get a great phone in 2026. Here are the best options under ₦100,000.

✍ NaijaBuzz Staff•May 2026•8 min read

In a country where the average monthly salary sits below ₦150,000, spending ₦500,000 on a flagship smartphone is simply not realistic for most people. But the good news is that in 2026, you do not need to spend half a million naira to get a great phone. The budget smartphone market has matured dramatically, and several devices under ₦100,000 now offer performance that would have cost three times as much just three years ago.

The Tecno Spark 30 Pro sits at the top of our list at around ₦75,000. It features a 6.8-inch AMOLED display, a 108MP main camera, and a 5,000mAh battery that comfortably lasts two full days. For Nigerians dealing with unreliable power supply, that battery life alone makes it worth considering. The Spark 30 Pro also handles mobile data efficiently — important when you are on a metered data plan.

The Infinix Hot 50 Pro at ₦68,000 is our pick for best camera in the budget category. Its AI-enhanced photography system produces surprisingly sharp images even in low light — the kind of night photography that previously required a midrange device. For content creators, small business owners posting products on Instagram, or anyone who takes a lot of photos, this is compelling value.

If performance is your priority, the Xiaomi Redmi 14C at ₦82,000 offers the most processing power in this price range. It runs on a Helio G91 chip that handles multitasking, mobile gaming, and video streaming without the frustrating lag that plagues cheaper devices. The Redmi also benefits from Xiaomi's MIUI software, which is feature-rich and regularly updated.

Our overall recommendation: if you want the best all-round experience under ₦100,000, the Tecno Spark 30 Pro wins. If camera quality is most important, go for the Infinix Hot 50 Pro. And if you need raw speed for work or gaming, the Xiaomi Redmi 14C is your best bet. Any of these three will serve you well for at least two to three years.

💡 Key Takeaway: Tecno Spark 30 Pro (₦75k), Infinix Hot 50 Pro (₦68k), and Xiaomi Redmi 14C (₦82k) are the top budget picks for 2026.
